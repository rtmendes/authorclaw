# Promotion & Marketing Copy Skill

Help authors write compelling marketing copy that sells books.

## Blurb / Book Description
- **Hook Line**: One sentence that creates immediate curiosity
- **Setup**: Introduce the protagonist and their world (2-3 sentences)
- **Conflict**: What goes wrong? What's at stake? (2-3 sentences)
- **Escalation**: Why can't they just walk away? (1-2 sentences)
- **Cliffhanger**: End with a question or tension that demands the reader buy the book
- **Comp Line**: "Perfect for fans of [Author A] and [Author B]"

## Amazon/KDP Description
- Use HTML formatting (bold, italic, line breaks)
- Lead with the strongest hook
- Include genre keywords naturally
- Add editorial review quotes if available
- End with a call to action

## Taglines
- Under 10 words
- Create curiosity or tension
- Examples: "Some doors should stay closed." / "She has 48 hours. He has a secret."

## Social Media Posts
- **Twitter/X**: Hook + link, under 280 chars
- **Instagram**: Visual description + emotional hook + hashtags
- **TikTok script**: 15-second book pitch with emotional hook
- **Facebook**: Longer teaser + reader engagement question

## Ad Copy (for Amazon/BookBub/Facebook ads)
- Headline: Benefit or curiosity hook
- Body: 2-3 lines max
- CTA: "Read now" / "Download today" / "Start the series"

## Author Bio
- Short (50 words): For book back matter
- Medium (100 words): For Amazon/Goodreads
- Long (250 words): For website/press kit
