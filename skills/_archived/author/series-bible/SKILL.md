# Series Bible Skill

Manage continuity across multiple books in a series.

## Series-Level Tracking

### Overarching Series Arc
- Series premise and central conflict
- How the series-level stakes escalate across books
- Series-level theme that deepens with each book
- Planned number of books and role of each

### Character Arcs Across Books
- Where each character starts (Book 1) and ends (final book)
- Growth milestones per book
- Relationship evolution between characters
- Characters introduced/killed per book

### World Evolution
- How the world changes across the series
- New locations, factions, or rules per book
- Technology or magic system evolution
- Political/social shifts

### Continuity Checklist Per Book
- ✅ Character ages updated correctly
- ✅ Scars, injuries, and physical changes carried forward
- ✅ Relationship status matches end of previous book
- ✅ World state matches end of previous book
- ✅ Unresolved plot threads from previous books addressed
- ✅ Foreshadowing from earlier books pays off
- ✅ No contradictions with established facts

### Reader Entry Points
- Can a new reader start at this book? If yes, what needs recapping?
- "Previously on..." summary for returning readers
- Standalone satisfaction vs. series cliffhanger balance
