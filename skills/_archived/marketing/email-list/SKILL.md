# Email List Marketing Skill

Help authors build and nurture their email list.

## Welcome Sequence (5-7 emails)
1. **Delivery** — Here's your free book/bonus + personal intro
2. **Story** — Your origin story as a writer (build connection)
3. **Value** — Exclusive content, sneak peek, or reading rec
4. **Engagement** — Ask a question, invite to reply
5. **Soft sell** — Introduce your books naturally
6. **Social proof** — Reader testimonials and reviews
7. **Direct ask** — "If you've been curious about [book], now's the time"

## Launch Sequence (7-10 emails over 2 weeks)
- Pre-launch tease → Cover reveal → Blurb reveal → 
- Early access/preorder → Launch day → Day 2 reminder → 
- Social proof → Last chance → Thank you + what's next

## Ongoing Newsletter Templates
- Behind-the-scenes writing updates
- Reading recommendations
- Exclusive short fiction or deleted scenes
- Fan Q&A responses
- New release announcements

## Subject Line Framework
- Curiosity: "I almost deleted this chapter"
- Benefit: "A free [genre] novella for you"
- Personal: "Something I've never told anyone"
- Urgency: "[Book] is 99¢ for 48 hours"
- Question: "What would you do if...?"
