# Social Media Marketing Skill

Create platform-specific promotional content for authors.

## Platform-Specific Content

### Twitter/X (280 chars)
- Hook + micro-pitch + link
- Quote from book (under 15 words) + "From my upcoming [genre]"
- Engagement: "What's your favorite [trope]?"
- Behind-the-scenes: Writing stats, process reveals

### Instagram / Bookstagram
- Aesthetic flat-lay caption ideas
- Carousel: "5 things about my protagonist"
- Story polls: "Which cover do you prefer?"
- Reel script: 15-second book pitch

### TikTok / BookTok
- Hook-first 3-second scripts
- "POV: You're reading [book] and..."
- Trope reveal videos
- Writing process content

### Facebook
- Longer narrative posts (personal story + book connection)
- Reader group engagement prompts
- Launch day templates

## Content Calendar
Help plan a 30-day launch strategy:
- Week 1-2: Tease and build anticipation
- Week 3: Reveal (cover, blurb, date)
- Week 4: Launch week (daily content)
