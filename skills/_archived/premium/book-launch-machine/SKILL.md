# Book Launch Machine — Premium Skill

A complete book launch system that generates everything you need from 30 days before launch to 30 days after.

## The 60-Day Launch Timeline

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Days -30 to -7)

**Week 1 (Days -30 to -24)**
Auto-generates:
- [ ] Cover reveal social posts (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok script)
- [ ] Cover reveal email to list
- [ ] Pre-order setup checklist (platform-specific)
- [ ] ARC team recruitment email
- [ ] Goodreads listing draft
- [ ] BookBub pre-order alert submission

**Week 2 (Days -23 to -17)**
Auto-generates:
- [ ] Blurb A/B test variants (5 versions)
- [ ] Amazon description (HTML formatted)
- [ ] Back cover copy
- [ ] "First chapter free" lead magnet version
- [ ] Social media teaser campaign (7 posts)
- [ ] Blog tour pitch email

**Week 3 (Days -16 to -10)**
Auto-generates:
- [ ] Launch team instructions email
- [ ] Review request templates (for ARCs)
- [ ] Amazon keyword research (7 keywords)
- [ ] BISAC category recommendations
- [ ] Newsletter swap pitches to 5 authors in genre
- [ ] Countdown social posts

**Week 4 (Days -9 to -1)**
Auto-generates:
- [ ] Launch day email sequence (3 emails)
- [ ] "The story behind the story" blog post
- [ ] Author Q&A social content
- [ ] Amazon AMS ad copy (10 variations)
- [ ] Facebook ad copy (5 variations)
- [ ] BookBub Featured Deal application

### Phase 2: Launch Week (Days 0 to 7)

**Launch Day (Day 0)**
Auto-generates:
- [ ] Launch announcement email
- [ ] Social blitz posts (every 2 hours, 6 posts)
- [ ] "Thank you" post for pre-order supporters
- [ ] Direct message templates for close contacts

**Days 1-3: Momentum Push**
- [ ] Review reminder to ARC team
- [ ] "Still climbing!" social updates
- [ ] Amazon rank celebration posts (if applicable)
- [ ] Cross-promotion posts with newsletter swap partners

**Days 4-7: Sustained Push**
- [ ] Mid-week email to list (social proof + reviews)
- [ ] "Last chance for launch price" if using intro pricing
- [ ] Blog tour follow-up
- [ ] Goodreads giveaway setup

### Phase 3: Post-Launch (Days 8 to 30)

Auto-generates:
- [ ] Post-launch analysis template
- [ ] "What I learned" social post
- [ ] Review response templates (for positive and critical reviews)
- [ ] Next book teaser strategy
- [ ] Ongoing ad optimization recommendations
- [ ] 30-day metrics tracking dashboard
